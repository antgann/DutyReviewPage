The map shows the regions which have alarms configured for which the duty seismologist will be paged. Clicking on one of the regions should launch a tooltip giving the name of the alarm region followed by a list of alarms, showing the time range and magnitude range.

One additional, larger-region alarm is not shown. If an event in the magnitude range 3.95-8.5 is triggered at any time in the latitude range +25 to +50, longitude range -100 to -200, a page will be sent to the duty seismologist.

The alarm settings are correct as of: 8 March 2017.
